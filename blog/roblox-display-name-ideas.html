Display Name vs Username: What Is the Difference?

Before we dive into the name ideas, let us clear up something that confuses a lot of players. Your Roblox username and your display name are two different things.

Your username is your permanent account identifier. It is what shows in your profile URL and what people use to search for you. Changing your username costs Robux and it is a one time change.

Your display name is the name that shows on your profile and above your avatar in games. You can change it every 7 days for free. Display names can include spaces and special characters that usernames cannot. This is why display names are so much more creative and expressive than usernames.

Most of the names in this guide are designed as display names since they are easier to change and allow more creative freedom.

How to Change Your Roblox Display Name

Changing your display name is simple and free. Here are the steps.

  1. Go to roblox.com/my/account and log in
  2. Click on the pencil icon next to your current display name
  3. Type your new display name in the field
  4. Click "Save" to confirm the change

Remember, you can only change your display name once every 7 days. So pick a name you will be happy with for at least a week. If you are not sure, keep reading because we have tons of ideas below.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Display Name

With so many options, choosing the right display name can feel overwhelming. Here are some guidelines to help you decide.

Match Your Name to Your Bio

Your display name and bio should tell the same story. A dark aesthetic name like "Eclipse" paired with a cute bio full of hearts feels disconnected. Keep everything cohesive for the best impression.

Match Your Name to Your Avatar

The same rule applies to your avatar. If your avatar is dressed in all pink, a name like "Dark Void" creates confusion. Names like "Rosie" or "Peachy Dreams" make much more sense visually.

Keep It Short

Shorter names look better above your avatar in game. They are also easier for other players to type in chat. Aim for one or two words maximum. Long display names get cut off on mobile devices.

Avoid Numbers and Random Characters

Names like "xXx_Shadow_xXx" or "Player12345" look dated and low effort. Clean, readable names always make a better impression. If your preferred name is taken, try a creative variation instead of adding numbers.

Check How It Looks

Before committing to a display name, consider how it will look in different contexts: above your avatar, in chat messages, on your profile page, and in friend lists. Some names look great in one place but awkward in another.

Think About Pronunciation

Other players will try to say your name in voice chat and conversation. A name that is hard to pronounce will just get shortened or replaced with a nickname. Simple, pronounceable names are always better.
