Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

BioBlox does not collect personal information. We do not require sign-ups, logins, or accounts. The only data used is the Roblox username you enter, which is used temporarily to fetch publicly available profile information.

2. How We Use Data

When you enter a Roblox username:

  • We fetch your public avatar, join date, and friend/follower counts via Roblox's public API
  • This data is used only to generate your flex card or bio
  • No data is stored on our servers — everything runs in your browser

3. Cookies & Tracking

BioBlox does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We may use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand page views and improve the Service. No personal data is tied to analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Roblox API — to fetch public profile data
  • Google Fonts — to load website fonts
  • Cloudflare — for CDN and hosting

5. Children's Privacy

BioBlox is a tool designed for Roblox players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is stored, there is minimal privacy risk.

6. Data Security

Since BioBlox processes everything client-side (in your browser), no user data is transmitted to or stored on our servers. Your Roblox username is only used in real-time API calls.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
